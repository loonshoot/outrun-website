# AI Integration

Connect AI models directly to your standardized data through secure protocols. Enable intelligent analysis and automated insights without complex custom integrations.

## Overview

AI integration transforms your workspace into an intelligent data platform. AI models can query, analyze, and interact with your standardized business data in real-time while maintaining security and compliance.

## How AI Integration Works

Your standardized data becomes accessible to AI models through secure, protocol-based connections. AI models automatically discover your data schema and relationships between People, Organizations, and Analytics, enabling contextual insights without manual configuration.
