Frequently asked questions

What counts as a sync?

A sync is counted every time data is moved between your connected apps. This includes imports, exports, real-time updates, and scheduled synchronizations.

How does custom pricing work?

We tailor pricing to your usage - the number of connectors, sync volume, and any specific requirements. Contact us and we’ll put together a quote that fits your needs.

What happens if my usage changes?

We’ll work with you to adjust your plan as your needs grow. No surprises - you’re always in control of your spend.

How do connectors work?

Each connector links to one data source (HubSpot, Zoho CRM, etc.). You can connect multiple instances of the same service - each counts as one connector.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. You only pay for your agreed plan. If you need hands-on onboarding help, we can discuss that separately.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Start with a free trial to connect your tools and see how Outrun works with your data. No credit card required.
