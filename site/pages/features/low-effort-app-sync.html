Feature

Low Effort App Sync

Connect your business tools and keep data flowing in both directions. Outrun pulls from your sources, standardises everything into a unified schema, and delivers it to your destinations — automatically, continuously, and without code.

Capabilities

Connect once, sync everything

Automated Field Mapping

Fields from every connected app are automatically mapped to standardised schemas using pre-built consolidation configs. No manual mapping required.

Bidirectional Sync

Data flows both ways. Pull contacts, deals, and activities from your sources, and push standardised records back to your CRM destinations.

Continuous Polling

Sources are polled on a configurable schedule, so new and updated records flow into your workspace automatically without manual intervention.
