How it works

Collect from everywhere, use it anywhere

Pull from every app

Connect your CRMs, productivity tools, and developer platforms. Outrun pulls contacts, deals, activities, and more from all of them automatically.

Clean and standardise

Every record is automatically mapped to a consistent format. A contact in HubSpot looks the same as one in Zoho or Pipedrive - no manual cleanup needed.

Use it right away

Browse, query, and push your clean data to any destination. Build workflows on top of it, run AI agents against it, or sync it to another CRM.

Automate your data cleanup

Once your data is inside Outrun, you can run workflows and AI agents to keep it clean. Scrape incoming emails for CRM updates, enrich contacts from external sources, flag duplicates, or build custom rules that maintain data quality over time. Use pre-built templates or create your own.

What gets standardised

Every app stores data differently. Outrun maps it all into these consistent data formats so you never have to think about field names or data structures again.

People & Companies

People (contacts, leads)
Organisations (companies, accounts)
Relationships (links between records)

Sales & Activity

Facts (deals, opportunities)
Activities (calls, emails, meetings)
Tasks (to-dos, follow-ups)

Communication & Productivity

Messages (emails, chats)
Cases (support tickets)
Conversations (threaded discussions)
Calendars & events
Files (documents, attachments)
Projects (workstreams, initiatives)
