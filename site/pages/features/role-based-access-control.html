Feature

Role-based Access Control

Control who can access your workspace and what they can do. Assign roles to team members so the right people have the right level of access - nothing more, nothing less.

View all features →

Capabilities

Simple roles, clear boundaries

Workspace Roles

Every team member gets a role - Owner, Admin, or Member. Each role has clear permissions so everyone knows what they can and cannot do.

Invite & Manage

Invite team members by email and assign their role on the spot. Change roles or remove access any time from your workspace settings.

Separated Workspaces

Each workspace is fully isolated. Team members only see data and settings for the workspaces they belong to.

What each role can do

Owner

Full access to all workspace settings
Manage billing & subscription
Invite and remove team members
Connect and disconnect integrations
Create and manage workflows

Admin

Connect and manage integrations
Create and manage workflows
Invite team members
View all synced data
Manage workspace settings

Member

View synced data
Browse contacts, companies & deals
View workflow run history
Use the data browser
Access their own settings

Keep your workspace secure as your team grows

As you add more people to Outrun, roles make sure everyone has the access they need - and nothing they do not. It is a simple system that scales with your team.

✓ Only Owners and Admins can change integrations and settings
✓ Members get read access to data without risk of accidental changes
✓ Billing is restricted to workspace Owners
✓ Workspace isolation keeps each team's data separate

Common questions

Can I change someone's role later?

Yes. Owners and Admins can update roles from workspace settings at any time.

How many members can I add?

There is no hard limit on team members. Add as many people as your plan allows.

Can someone be in multiple workspaces?

Yes. A single account can belong to multiple workspaces, each with its own role.

What happens when I remove someone?

They immediately lose access to the workspace. No data is deleted.
