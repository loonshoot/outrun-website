Capabilities

AI-powered email workflows, ready to deploy

Email Triage

Incoming emails are automatically sent to AI for classification by priority level (urgent, high, medium, low) and category (sales, support, billing, internal), with automatic action item extraction.

Daily Digest

On a daily schedule, all emails received in the period are compiled, triaged, and summarized by AI, then delivered as a single consolidated digest email with priority groupings.

Vendor Change Tracker

Scheduled scans detect vendor notification emails, analyze them for codebase impact, and automatically create GitHub issues with the relevant context so your engineering team stays ahead of breaking changes.
