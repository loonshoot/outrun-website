Your data shouldn't live in just one place

SaaS tools are great - until someone accidentally deletes a field, a bad import overwrites your records, or a vendor has an outage. Having an independent copy of your data means you can always recover.

✓ Recover from accidental deletions or overwrites
✓ Catch bad data imports before they cause damage
✓ Keep working even when your vendor is down
✓ Full change history so you know what happened and when

Independent Copy
