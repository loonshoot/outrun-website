Feature

Data Browser

Browse and search the data Outrun syncs from your tools. See your contacts, companies, deals, and activities all in one place - no switching between apps.

View all features →

Capabilities

Explore your synced data in one place

Search & Filter

Find any record across all your connected tools. Search by name, email, deal stage, or any other field to quickly locate what you need.

Collection Overview

See how much data has been synced from each tool at a glance. View record counts and collection details for every connected source.

Tabbed Navigation

Switch between People, Companies, Deals, and Activities with simple tabs. Each view is tailored to the data type you are exploring.

What you can browse

• Contacts and people from your CRM
• Companies and organisations
• Deals and opportunities
• Activities like emails, calls, and meetings
• Data from every connected source
• Standardised fields across different tools
• Record details and raw data
• Works on desktop and mobile

Stop switching between apps to find what you need

Once Outrun syncs your tools, the Data Browser gives you a single place to look up any contact, company, or deal. No more logging into three different CRMs to find a phone number.

✓ See all your synced data without leaving Outrun
✓ Quickly look up contacts, companies, and deals
✓ Understand what data has been synced and from where
✓ Consistent view regardless of which tool the data came from

How it works

Connect Your Tools

Link your CRM, email, and other tools to Outrun. Data syncs automatically.

Browse by Category

Use tabs to switch between People, Companies, Deals, and Activities.

Search & Filter

Find specific records with search, or filter by source to see data from a particular tool.

View Record Details

Click any record to see its full details, including which tool it was synced from.
