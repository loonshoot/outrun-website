Feature

Tool Migration

Migrate your data between any tools that Outrun connects to. Moving CRMs? Switching sales tools? Outrun helps you get your data from A to B with your contacts, deals, and history intact.

Capabilities

Switch tools without losing your data

Full Data Export

Pull your data out of your current tool with contacts, deals, notes, and activity history preserved.

Field Mapping

Outrun maps fields between your source and destination tools using built-in mappings for supported connectors. Review and adjust before migrating.

Run in Parallel

Keep using your current tool while migrating data to the new one. Transition at your own pace without disrupting your team.

Supported migration paths

CRM to CRM

Move between HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, Salesforce, or Zendesk Sell. Contacts, deals, companies, and activity history come with you.

Any Supported Connector
